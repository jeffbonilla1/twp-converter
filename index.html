Before You Upload to KDP — Verify Your EPUB
Always proof your epub before publishing. The most common issue: a paragraph accidentally styled as Heading 1 in Word will appear as a false chapter break. Check the chapter list above first, then verify the full file using one of these methods.
01
Send to Your Kindle App
The closest thing to seeing your finished book. Email the .epub as an attachment to your Send-to-Kindle address — find it at amazon.com → Account → Content & Devices → Devices. Your sending address must be on the Approved Senders list under Preferences → Personal Document Settings. Allow 2–5 minutes after sending, then sync your Kindle app.
02
EPUBReader — In Your Browser
Fastest option, no install needed. Go to epubread.com, upload your file, and it renders instantly. Good for a quick chapter structure check. Also available as a Chrome extension if you prefer reading locally.
03
Kindle Previewer — See It Before KDP Does
Amazon's own free desktop tool shows you exactly how your book will look to readers before you publish. Download at amazon.com/kindlepreviewer. Open your .epub, flip through every chapter, and check the auto-generated table of contents. If it looks right here, it will look right on KDP.
!
Fixing Chapter Errors in Word
If a section is showing up as a false chapter, open your .docx in Word, click on the problem paragraph, and check the Styles panel in the Home tab. It will show "Heading 1" — change it to "Normal" or the appropriate body style, save the file, and re-upload here.
